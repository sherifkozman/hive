# Task: internal-comms / BROAD

The company (SaaS, ~800 employees, hybrid) is changing its expense policy:
per-diem meal allowances replace itemized meal receipts for trips under 5
days (US$75/day domestic, US$110/day international); alcohol no longer
reimbursable; booking through the corporate travel tool becomes mandatory for
airfare (personal-card bookings reimbursed only with pre-approval); changes
effective the 1st of next month; finance ran a 2-month pilot with the sales
org — 40% faster reimbursements, 92% satisfaction; old expense reports
submitted within 30 days of travel still honored during a 60-day transition.

Produce the complete internal communications package:
1. All-hands announcement (email from CFO) — the primary artifact.
2. Slack #announcements short version linking to the email.
3. Manager talking points (for team leads fielding questions).
4. FAQ (at least 8 questions employees will actually ask, with answers
   derivable from the facts above; where facts are missing, say what the
   policy team must decide rather than inventing).

Tone: clear, human, no corporate mush; change framed honestly (some people
lose flexibility) with the pilot data used credibly, not as spin.
