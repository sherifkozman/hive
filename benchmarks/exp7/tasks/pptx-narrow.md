# Task: pptx / NARROW

A user has `template.pptx` (their company's branded deck template) and wants a
5-slide quarterly business review generated from bullet-point content they
provide, keeping the template's branding, layouts, fonts, and colors intact.

Produce the complete recommended workflow and code/tooling steps to do this
correctly: how to inventory the template's layouts and placeholders, how to
duplicate and populate slides without breaking branding, what to avoid
editing directly, and how to verify the output renders correctly before
delivery. Include concrete commands/code for each step and a QA checklist.

Faithfulness to the recommended template-editing workflow (vs naive
from-scratch generation) is the primary grading criterion.
