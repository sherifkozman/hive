# T3 — financial-analysis

Using `eval3/fixtures/quarterly_financials.csv` (historical quarterly revenue
and EBIT) plus these assumptions — WACC 10%, terminal growth 2.5%, tax 25%,
D&A 3% of revenue, capex 4% of revenue, NWC change 5% of incremental revenue,
5-year projection with revenue growth starting at trailing-year growth and
fading linearly to 10% — produce a concise DCF: projected FCFF table, terminal
value, enterprise value. Show your derivation of the trailing-year growth rate
from the historicals. You may run Python; arithmetic must be correct. Skill
domains available: `skills/financial-analysis/composable/` and
`skills/data-analysis/composable/`.
